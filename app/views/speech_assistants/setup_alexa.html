Connect Alexa with your Z-Way Smart Home

Z-Way Smart Home can connect with Amazon Alexa devices to give you command of your home through voice controls. This guide help you to setup the Amazon Alexa "Z-Way Smart Home" skill and give you additional infomrations for configuring your devices.

Get started

Before we can start, please setup your Amazon Alexa device. You can get additional information here:
  • Set up your Amazon Alexa device
  • Download the Amazon Alexa mobile app or
  • Use the Amazon Alexa web app

Login to Alexa

To activate then "Z-Way Smart Home" Skill you have to login first to the Amazon Alexa site.
Open the Amazon Alexa site and login.

Found the "Z-Way Smart Home" skill

  1. Select "Skills" form the menu left.
  2. Search for "Z-Way Smart Home" in the search field.
  3. Select "Z-Way Smart Home" skill

Enable the "Z-Way Smart Home" skill

Click the activate button to enable the skill. By enabling, the skill get access to all your available smart devices after authenticate them.

Enable the "Z-Way Smart Home" skill

Enter your "Remote ID / Username" and your password and click sign in

List and edit devices for Alexa support

After Alex has been successfully linked with your Z-Way Smart Home, you just have to activate your devices for Alexa. Click this Mange Alexa
or got to Devices > Speech assistants > Manage > Alexa > Gear wheel.
On this page you can add devcies to Alexa and give the devices a call name, a phonetic naming for simpler voice control.

Edit device in deive detail view

You can also activate or change the call name of a device in the device detail view.
Elements > widget configuration wheel > Detail/Konfiguration view
